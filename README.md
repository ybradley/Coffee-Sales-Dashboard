# Coffee-Sales-Dashboard

Coffee Company wants to create an interactive Dashboard so that stakeholders can understand their customers, loyalty to their brand of coffee, and summery of sales to make data driven decisions that will help them grow in current market.

## Questions (KPIs)

-Compare the sales and orders.

-Which month and year got the highest sales.

-Which country is purchasing more?

-List of top 5 customers.

-Relation between product type, size, loyalty and sales.

-Highest selling category?

## Process

-Verified data for any missing values and anomalies.

-Made sure data is consistent and clean with respect to data type, data format and values used.

-Created pivot tables according to the questions asked.

-Merged all pivot tables into one dashboard and applied slicers to make it dynamic.

## Project Insights

After cleaning and preparing the dataset, I created pivot tables and visualizations to answer key stakeholder questions:

Identified the total sales per country. 

This highlights which markets contribute the most revenue and where growth opportunities exist. Highest sales are in United States.


![country sum of sales](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d1e3ce50-9952-4e75-bd23-bf1a856e7b03)


Customer Loyalty (Top 5 Customers)

Ranked the top 5 customers by total purchases.

This shows who our most valuable customers are and helps with loyalty/retention strategies.


![top 5 customers](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e0d2b7c5-a206-4652-aa96-8f42d075da6b)


Coffee Sales by Type, Year, and Month.

Broke down sales by coffee type across years and months.

This revealed seasonality trends, and which coffee types drive the most revenue - February of 2020 Liberica coffee had highest sales.


![total sales](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ac275aee-bc8a-42d7-b1fb-3f2d55e03c62)


Visualizations and pivot tables make this insights easy to interpret and help stakeholders quickly understand business performance.

Additionally, I built an interactive dashboard with slicers, allowing stakeholders to filter sales by order date, order size, loyalty card and roast type name.

Stakeholders can explore the data independently and answer follow-up questions without needing to request additional analysis.
